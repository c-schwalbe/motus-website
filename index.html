The Toolkit

Native parametric tools designed to bypass UI limitations and interact directly with the API.

  • 01 IsolateWarnings One click tool to isolate / unisolate the view to show all elements containing a warning.
  • 02 CleanViews Easily remove orphaned elevation tags.
  • 03 MegaPurge Purge elements such as Views, Sheets, Links, CAD, and Raster Images that the built-in purge does not .
  • 04 DuplicateSheet Bi-directional Excel sync for room data sheets and finish schedules.
  • 05 CapitalizeNames Capitalize all view names that have been placed on a sheet.
  • 06 ElevateRoom Automatically create 4 interior elevations for selected rooms.
  • 07 ViewDirection Change the neame of views to match their orientation to the cardinal points of the project.
  • 09 NumberByClick Renumber problematic elements such as gridlines by clicking on or selecting them in sequence.
  • 10 DoorNumber Automatically renumber the doors based on their association to rooms and room numbers.
  • 11 WindowNumber Automatically renumber the windows based on their association to rooms and room numbers.
  • 12 Sum Add together the length of multiple selected lines or the area of multiple selected filled regions.
  • 13 RoomFinish Automatically add finish walls to selected rooms with inputs for height and wall type.
  • 14 LevelRt / LevelLt Filp the heads of levels to match either all left or all right.
  • 15 LoadDirectory Load all .rfa files in a file, includeing subfiles.
  • 16 RandomRotateTrees Rotate all of the planting elements in the file randomly.
  • 17 DimensionGrids Add overall and intermittent dimensions for all parallel grids in view.
